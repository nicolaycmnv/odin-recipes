Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish known for its layers of pasta, rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel sauce, flavorful meat, and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (either pre-cooked or oven-ready)
  • Tomato sauce (made with tomatoes, onions, garlic, herbs such as basil and oregano, and sometimes ground meat like beef or sausage)
  • Béchamel sauce (made with butter, flour, milk, salt, and sometimes nutmeg)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese and shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Optional: cooked ground beef, sausage, or vegetables such as spinach or mushrooms

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the temperature specified in your recipe.
  2. Prepare the tomato sauce by sautéing onions and garlic in olive oil until softened. Add crushed tomatoes and seasonings, and simmer until the sauce thickens. If using meat, brown it separately and then add it to the sauce.
  3. Prepare the béchamel sauce by melting butter in a saucepan, whisking in flour until smooth, then gradually adding milk while stirring constantly. Cook until thickened, and season with salt and nutmeg if desired.
  4. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions, or use oven-ready noodles if preferred.c
  5. Assemble the lasagna in a large baking dish by spreading a thin layer of tomato sauce on the bottom, followed by a layer of lasagna noodles.
  6. Add a layer of béchamel sauce on top of the noodles, followed by a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese and shredded mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.
  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 30-40 minutes, or until the lasagna is bubbling and the cheese is melted and golden brown.
  9. Let the lasagna rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Garnish with fresh herbs like basil if desired.
