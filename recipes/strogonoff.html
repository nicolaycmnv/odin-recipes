Stroganoff Recipe

Description

Stroganoff chicken is a delicious dish made with tender chicken pieces cooked in a creamy sauce, typically served with rice or pasta.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast or thigh, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Mushrooms, sliced (optional)
  • Butter or oil for cooking
  • Sour cream or heavy cream
  • Tomato paste or diced tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Paprika (optional)
  • Worcestershire sauce (optional)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet or frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pan and saut√© until softened and fragrant.
  3. Add the chicken pieces to the pan and cook until they are lightly browned on all sides and cooked through.
  4. If using mushrooms, add them to the pan and cook until they are softened.
  5. Stir in the tomato paste or diced tomatoes, along with any optional ingredients such as paprika or Worcestershire sauce.
  6. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the sour cream or heavy cream, stirring until everything is well combined and the sauce is heated through.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve the stroganoff chicken hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley if desired, and accompanied by rice, pasta, or your favorite side dish.
